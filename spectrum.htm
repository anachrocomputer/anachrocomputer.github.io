Sinclair Spectrum

The Sinclair Spectrum is a Z80-based machine with 48k of RAM. I have four examples of this machine, one of them in its original box. Only one of the four machines works, and that is the rattiest-looking one.

I also have an Opus Discovery 1 disk drive unit for the Spectrum.
