ICL One Per Desk

HOME > Computers > OPD

The ICL One Per Desk is an optimistically-named combination of a computer and a telephone. The computer section is based on the Motorola 68008, in a design that is very similar to the Sinclair QL. The machine has a pair of Microdrives, although they are incompatible with the Sinclair Microdrives due to a change in the recording format.
