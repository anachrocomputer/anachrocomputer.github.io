Dragon 32

HOME > Computers > Dragon 32

The Dragon 32 is a 6809-based machine with 32k of RAM. I have two examples of this machine, one of them in its original box. That one is the earlier type, without an additional space for the serial port connector. My second machine has the later plastic moulding for the lower half of the case, which does have the extra space. Since only the Dragon 64 had the actual serial port, my machine has the space blanked off.

I've also got a Trojan light pen for the Dragon, three game ROM cartridges (one shown above) and a number of game cassettes. I didn't get any joysticks with the machines, so I'll be modifying an old PC analog joystick to suit the Dragon's requirements.
