Repairing The DVD Drive in an Apple eMac

About the Machine

The Apple eMac is one of the "New World" Macs that were released following the success of the iMac. This particular machine was a 700MHz, Revision 1 eMac. It had the higher spec DVD/CDR drive fitted, which should have been capable of reading DVDs and burning CDs.

The Fault

My friend's eMac had a defective DVD/CDR drive. The only option was to remove and replace it, but first we had to get the eMac open and dig down to the drive.

The Fix

We obtained a replacement drive from somewhere; I think it was from FreeCycle. We actually fitted a plain DVD drive, because that's what we had.

Photos of the disassembly are in this Flickr set.

Reassembly is the reverse of disassembly.
