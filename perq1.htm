Perq

HOME > Computers > Perq

The ICL Perq is an early example of a single user workstation machine. It has a monochrome bit-mapped screen in portrait orientation. The built-in hard disk is a 14" diameter type with mains motor and belt drive. Underneath the keyboard there is a seven-segment LED display that shows the machine's progress through the stages of booting up.
